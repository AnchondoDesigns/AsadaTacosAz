One grill

Seared over mesquite.

Asada Tacos began the way the best ones do — feeding friends, then friends of friends, until the line wrapped the parking lot. No storefront. No franchise playbook. Just skirt steak marinated overnight, seared hard over mesquite, and folded into hand-pressed corn.

We kept the menu short on purpose. When the asada is right, it doesn't need help. That discipline is what turned a weekend pop-up into a licensed, insured operation that thousands of Valley regulars now plan their week around.

Today you'll find us rotating through East Valley taprooms — because great tacos and a cold local pour belong together. And when there's something worth celebrating, we load the grill and bring the whole experience to you.

The name is a standard we hold ourselves to. Sabor original — the original flavor — earned one plate at a time.
